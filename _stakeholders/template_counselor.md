## Profile

Licensed psychologists, therapists, or counselors working in university counseling centers or student wellness services.

## Areas of activity

* Review AI-generated guidance
* Provide evidence-based coping techniques
* Recommend professional intervention when necessary
* Contribute educational content
* Analyze anonymized well-being trends (with consent)


## Relevance as a stakeholder

Their expertise is essential for ensuring that the application promotes responsible mental health practices and avoids harmful or misleading advice.
