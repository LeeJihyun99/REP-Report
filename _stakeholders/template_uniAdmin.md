## Profile

University management, student affairs office, or wellness program coordinators responsible for student support initiatives.

## Areas of activity

* Promote the application to students
* Allocate funding and resources
* Review anonymized usage statistics
* Evaluate project success
* Coordinate with counseling services



## Relevance as a stakeholder

Their support determines whether the application will be adopted, maintained, and expanded as part of the university's mental health initiatives.
