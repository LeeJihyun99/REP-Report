## Profile

University students experiencing stress, burnout, anxiety, or exam-related pressure who seek accessible mental health support.


## Areas of activity

* Register and manage an account
* Complete daily mood check-ins
* Chat with the AI assistant
* Track emotional progress
* Receive personalized coping strategies and reminders
* Access emergency mental health resources


## Relevance as a Stakeholder

The application is designed specifically for students; therefore, they are the most important stakeholder. Their satisfaction determines whether the system achieves its purpose of improving student well-being.
