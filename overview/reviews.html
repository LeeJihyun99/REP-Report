Reviews ({{ collection.size }})

Reviews are used for requirements validation and try to identify and capture errors and issues. Three types of reviews can be distinguished:

  • Commenting where typically co-workers review the requirements and try to identify issues.
  • Inspections are rather formal and follow a strict protocoll to check artefacts for errors.
  • Walk-throughs are a light-weight version of an inspection.
As methods, typically, perspective-based reading, validation through prototypes or checklists are used.
